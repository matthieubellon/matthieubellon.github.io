WTO announces outcome of 2016 Essay Award for Young Economists, 2016, link The winner of the 2016 WTO Essay Award for Young Economists is Matthieu Bellon of Columbia University. His paper, entitled “Trade Liberalization and Inequality: a Dynamic Model with Firm and Worker Heterogeneity”, was ranked first by the Selection Panel. He receives a prize of CHF 5,000. The panel also awarded an honourable mention to Eunhee Lee of Yale University for her work entitled “Trade, Inequality, and the Endogenous Sorting of Heterogeneous Workers”.
